"There is pleasure in the pathless woods, there is rapture in the lonely shore, there is society where none intrudes, by the deep sea, and music in its roar; I love not Man the less, but Nature more." -Lord Bryon

It is important to remember that our "pale blue dot" is the only place we can call home for now. Even when we redefine what we know as home, it is important that we conserve our resources. If we over-exploit what we have on Earth, what stops us from over-exploiting resources of our new frontiers?

Maybe at this point you want to know how you can help out. One of the simplest ways to help is to pick up after yourself. In addition, there's many organizations specifically setup to preserve what we have. Some of these organizations are The Nature Conservancy, The Rainforest Alliance, Fauna and Flora International, American Rivers, and the Marine Conservation Society to name a few. You can make a donation to any of these organizations on their websites.
