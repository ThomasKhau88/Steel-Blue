Food Around The World

Italy

Italy is mainly known for the delicious pizza and pasta. The most famous pasta from Italy is penne and the most famous sauce is bolognese. The most popular pizza is margherita. If you click the button below, it will show you pictures of Italian food.

China

The chinese cuisine is very popular among many countries. Some popular dishes are General Tso's chicken, egg rolls, and fried rice.

Greece

Greece has amazing dishes consisting of Moussaka and Souvlaki.

Mexico

Obviously, tacos and burritos are staples in Mexico, but a very popular breakfast dish is chilaquiles. Chilaquiles is toasted corn tortilla with hot sauce, chicken, chorizo, shredded beef, and an egg on top.
